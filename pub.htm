OUR VOICE MAGAZINE

Our Voice is a digital magazine that offers LGBT+ people a place to discuss their own community and issues. Bodie was a contributor to the fifth issue of the magazine, tasked with researching and writing an educational article about transgender hormone replacement therapy.

This issue is still in publication, but a link will be posted upon its completion.

THE FRIGHTFUL DEGRADATION OF MANHOOD

Bodie's undergraduate honors thesis was an 80-page research paper exploring the relationship between Victorian discourses on masculinity and three works of popular fiction written in England in the 1880s and 90s. This project was was awarded honors by the Wesleyan University English department.
